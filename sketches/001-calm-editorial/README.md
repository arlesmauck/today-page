## Variant: Calm Editorial

### Design stance
A digital morning newspaper. Slow, intentional, and generous with whitespace. You read one story at a time. The typography is large and serif-driven, evoking print journalism. The pace is deliberately unhurried.

### Key choices
- **Layout:** Single column, centered, max-width 42rem. Comfortable margins.
- **Typography:** Crimson Text (serif) for headlines and body; Inter (sans) for metadata and UI chrome.
- **Color:** Warm off-white background (#f7f5f0) with dark gray text. No pure black. Muted, ink-like feel.
- **Interaction:** Tabbed news categories (World, Tech, Local, Reddit). Hover states on headlines. Expandable calendar.

### Trade-offs
- **Strong at:** Reading experience, reducing anxiety, feeling "finished" rather than infinitely scrolling.
- **Weak at:** Glance-ability. You have to scroll and read. Not ideal for "I'm running late."

### Best for
- Mornings when you have 10-15 minutes to actually read.
- People who want to feel informed, not merely notified.
